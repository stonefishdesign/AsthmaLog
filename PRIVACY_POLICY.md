# AsthmaLog Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2, 2026*

This Privacy Policy describes how AsthmaLog collects, uses, and protects your personal and health information when you use our asthma tracking application.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Local Access Credentials:** Email address and password used solely for app access control (stored locally on your device).
- **Profile Data:** Profile names, profile images, and user preferences you create within the app.
- **Health Data:** Daily logs, triggers, and medication usage entered by the user into the app.
- **Medical Information:** Emergency contacts, doctor information, and medical card details you choose to store.
- **App Usage Data:** Settings, reminders, and notification preferences to improve your experience.
- **Action Plan Data:** Action plans created and stored within the app.
- **Voice Logs (Optional):** If you use the voice logging feature, audio recordings are temporarily captured to extract your log data. The audio is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing and is not stored by AsthmaLog after extraction is complete.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide personalized asthma tracking and medication reminders.
- To generate data exports and reports for your personal records or healthcare provider.
- To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
- **Note:** We do not track user behavior or collect anonymous analytics.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- Your health records and logs are stored locally on your device. We do not use external servers or cloud services for continuous data storage.
- **Voice Logging Exception:** If you enable voice logging, your audio recordings are sent to Google's Gemini API solely for transcription and structured data extraction. Google's API usage is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Audio is not permanently stored by AsthmaLog and is deleted from your device after processing.
- Your data remains under your complete control and is not accessible to third parties.
- Data security relies on your device's built-in security features (Passcode, FaceID/TouchID).

## 4. Data Sharing and Export

- Your health data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
- You can export your data as CSV files to share with healthcare providers or for personal records.
- We do not sell, rent, or monetize your personal or health information.
- No advertising networks or analytics services have access to your health data.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

- You can view, edit, or delete any information you've entered at any time.
- You can create multiple profiles for family members while keeping data separate.
- You can disable notifications and reminders through app settings.
- You can delete the app to remove all data from your device.
- You can enable or disable the voice logging feature at any time in Settings.
- You have full control over what data to include in exports.

## 6. Children's Privacy

- AsthmaLog can be used by parents to track their children's asthma data through separate profiles.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
- Parents maintain full control over their children's health data within the app.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
- Significant changes will be communicated through app notifications or updates.
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Contact Us

- If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at [support@AsthmaLog.com](mailto:support@AsthmaLog.com).
- For technical support or app-related issues, use the Help & Support feature in the app.
